# Night Hours

Subtitle: — a small, glowing confession.

Content is sourced from `RESUME.nighthours`:

> This part of the junkyard only lights up after the rest has been turned over — fitting, because the best of my work usually happens at the same time.

## Reasons

- **Quiet** — No Slack, no meetings, no half-conversations bleeding into the focus.
- **Non-linear thinking** — Easier to hold the whole problem in your head without something pulling on a sleeve.
- **Permission to follow the thread** — When nobody is waiting on you, a curious tangent at 1am has time to become a feature by morning.
- **The lantern stays on** — Coffee, lo-fi, soldering iron warming up. The cat in the windowsill, sometimes.

> Most of what's in this junkyard was built between 22:00 and 02:00.
