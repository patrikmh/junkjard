# Work Style, Approach, and Philosophy

## How Patrik Works
- **Night owl:** Most deep work happens between 22:00 and 02:00. Quiet, uninterrupted, linear thinking time.
- **Tinkerer-first:** Patrik learns by building. Every new technology gets a small side project before production use.
- **Agentic mindset:** Believes the future of software development is human-in-the-loop with agent swarms, not pure manual coding.
- **Grounded in science:** MSc Cognitive Neuroscience + BSc Psychology means experimental design, statistical rigour, and skepticism of correlation-as-causation.

## Technical Philosophy
- **Agents are scaffolding, not magic:** The model is the cheap part. The real work is in the wiring — tools, memory, guardrails, feedback loops.
- **RAG needs a librarian:** Vector search alone isn't enough. You need grounding checks, self-critique, and someone (or something) curating the knowledge base.
- **Edge ML is underrated:** Running inference on-device changes the privacy and latency equation completely. Patrik pushes for on-device where possible.
- **Plain language over buzzwords:** Communicates to both technical and non-technical stakeholders without diluting accuracy.

## Collaboration Style
- Prefers hybrid / remote with focused async work and short synchronous check-ins.
- Enjoys cross-functional collaboration — has worked with embedded SW teams, marketers, product managers, and safety researchers.
- Comfortable being the "data person who can also deploy" — end-to-end ownership from notebook to production.

## What Patrik Is Looking For
- Roles that blend ML engineering, agentic AI, and product impact.
- Teams that value experimentation and tolerate the occasional 1am tangent that turns into a feature.
- Opportunities to work on RAG, multi-agent systems, or edge AI in production.
- Göteborg-based or remote-EU positions.
